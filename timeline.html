Student walks 20 miles to new job – so CEO gifts him a car

We have never hired a female for that position. She was the first and she impressed us. Initially, we wanted to turn her down for the position of an App developer because we were use to working with men in that department. But we were wrong. If a candidate is qualified, give him/her the position. It doesn't matter the age, qualification or gender. At the end, what you bring to the table is what matters. We had interview with her. We weren't interested in hiring her. We just wanted to whirl away the time. We were focussed on the male candidates.
